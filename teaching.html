Teaching

I have accumulated nearly ten years of teaching experience across both China and the United States. At the University of Georgia, I have served as the instructor of record for two courses: Data Analytics for International Policy and Chinese Politics. Prior to beginning my doctoral studies, I spent seven years as an assistant professor at a teaching-focused college in China, where I taught courses including Principles of Economics, Financial Analysis, and Online Marketing. My teaching background also includes experience as a teaching assistant of courses Introduction to International Relations, American Government, and Introduction to Comparative Politics.
